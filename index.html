About Me

I was born on July 2, 1995. Since that day, I've done everything in my power to learn as much as I possibly can in subjects that interest me. At the age of 15, I could play 13 instruments fluently, almost like music was just second nature to me. Then I learned to produce music and I've helped several artists publish over 20 songs so far.

After that, I discovered a new-found interest in 3D animating. I haven't published any animations yet but I have spent months playing around with the programs so I could fully understand everything that went into making animations. In the program, there is an add-on called MustardUI, which allows you to do certain things in the program without writing any new code. I wanted to know how it worked so bad. So I opened the .py to see for myself.

Which brings us to today! I understood absolutely nothing about the coding that went into the add-on. So I started browsing the internet to find out what was happening. That's where I discovered Thinkful. I'm to grow my knowledge in coding so that I can be a web developer and software engineer in the future.
